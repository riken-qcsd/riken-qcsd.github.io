The Quantum Computer System Design Research Team is recruiting people who are interested in working with us on research and development to realize fault-tolerant quantum computers.  

## Open Positions

RIKEN recruits individuals who have recently obtained a Ph.D., or who expect to obtain one soon, as Special Postdoctoral Researchers (SPDR). In addition to a salary, SPDR positions come with an individual research budget. If you are interested in a research career, please consider this opportunity. For details, please see the [SPDR program page](https://www.riken.jp/en/careers/programs/spdr/index.html). If you plan to apply, please contact the faculty member in advance.
